Inspiring message from the Director


Dear Future Enginners,

As the Director of the School of Automobile, Mechanical and Mechatronics Engineering, I'm here to inspire. You're the driving force in these fields of innovation, with the power to shape industries and fuel progress. Embrace challenges as stepping stones, merging theory with hands-on experience, and keep ethics at the core of your ambitions. Collaborate relentlessly, for your dreams are boundless and achievable, with the support of faculty, peers, and industry. Here's a straightforward plan to maximize your time with us:

  • Foundational Knowledge: Master engineering basics and develop strong study habits.
  • Passion Discovery: Join groups, workshops, and projects to find your interests.
  • Hands-On Learning: Apply theory in real-world situations through internships and projects.
  • Network Building: Connect with professors, professionals, and alumni.
  • Stay Current: Attend industry events and keep learning about the latest advancements.
  • Showcase Your Skills: Create a standout project demonstrating your abilities.
  • Professional Portfolio: Develop a strong resume and use your network for opportunities.

Remember, success lies not only in destination but in the journey itself. Believe in your potential, work hard, and transform your aspirations into reality at our institution, your launchpad to a remarkable future.

Best regards,
